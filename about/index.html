Hello, I’m Bogdan. I’m a student of Faculty of organization and informatics (FOI) located in Vara≈ædin, Croatia, and I consider myself to be a cybersecurity enthusiast.

My interests in informatics began in primary school where we have studied Terrapin logo and Basic programming languages. As such, when I joined high school I began attending informatics competitions. After high school I enrolled at FOI, which I’m still attending, and where I have graduated with a bachelors.

At the end of my second year I found my inclination towards networking and information security, and thus it became my field of interest. On my fourth year I joined a CTF1-solving team, formed by other students of FOI, called YoungPwnawans. Afterwards, I was introduced to the Open Systems and Security lab at FOI where I met some truly inspiring people and where we share our knowledge and skills.

The goal of this site is to present the stuff that I find interesting, work that I have done, CTF writeups, code, explanations and and anything considered worthy of sharing.

Besides IT-related stuff my hobbies include photography, photo and video editing, videogames and playing the guitar.
